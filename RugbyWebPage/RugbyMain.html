What is the Guinness Six Nations?

The Six Nations is a rugby event that happens once a year taking place in between around the 5th of February and ends around the 19th of March. Six teams par take in this sporting event, Ireland, England, France, Scotland, Wales and Italy. Each team must play each other once, building up points, untill each team has played each other once. Which ever team has the most points at the end wins the tournement.

When did the Guinness Six Nations Begin?

The competition originally began as the 4 nations as Italy and France had not yet joined. It began in 1883 called the Home Nations Championship. In 1910 France joined making it the 5 Nations and its stayed like this untill 2000 when Italy joined making it become todays name the 6 Nations.
